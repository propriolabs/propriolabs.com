Proprio Labs

Proprio is short for proprioception, humans' unconscious perception of movement and spatial orientation of the body. Proprio Labs is developing a platform to transform sensor data from wearable devices into performance metrics. Our goal is to provide the opportunity for anyone from an amateur athletes to a physical therapy patient to quantitatively track his or her performance with meaningful metrics.

X-Sense


X-Sense refers to our upcoming line of performance tracking products. We are building general software for the Apple Watch, Android Wear, and other IoT devices, and we are developing specific machine learning algorithms to identify metrics relevant to each sport. Our first product focuses on tennis performance metrics, and we'll have more to come soon!
